BodyIQ – App Support

Welcome to the official support page for BodyIQ. Here you can find help, troubleshooting tips, and ways to contact us if you experience any issues.

Common Issues

  • Subscription Access: Make sure you are logged in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
  • Restore Purchases: Go to the app’s settings and select Restore Purchases if you reinstalled the app.
  • App Crashes: Please ensure you are running the latest version of iOS and BodyIQ.

FAQs

Q: How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to update or cancel.

Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel?
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Please visit Apple’s support site.
