Privacy Policy

Football IQ – Mental Performance Training App  ·  Effective: June 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains in plain language what data Football IQ collects, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it short on purpose.

Who We Are

Football IQ is a mobile application designed to train football decision-making and tactical intelligence. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@footballiq.app.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the app:

  • Account info – name, email address, and password when you sign up.
  • Profile data – your position, age group, and skill level (chosen during onboarding).
  • Training data – your drill results, IQ scores, decision times, and progress through levels.
  • Device info – device type, OS version, and app version for bug fixing.
  • Usage data – which features you use and how often, to improve the experience.

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or microphone unless you explicitly use the match upload feature.

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalise your training pathway and IQ score.
  • To track your progress and show leaderboard rankings.
  • To improve app features and fix bugs.
  • To send you training reminders (only if you opt in).
  • To process payments for Premium subscriptions.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Sharing

We only share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the app — such as our cloud hosting provider and payment processor. These providers are contractually required to protect your data and cannot use it for their own purposes.

We may share anonymised, aggregated statistics (e.g. average IQ scores by position) publicly for research or marketing — this data cannot identify you.

Children's Privacy

Football IQ welcomes players aged 13 and above. For users under 16, we require parental or guardian consent before account creation. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure, but we follow industry best practices to protect your information.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time.
  • Export your training history.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@footballiq.app.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics tools (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel) to understand app usage. These tools may set cookies or similar identifiers. You can limit tracking through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via the app or by email before they take effect. The "Effective" date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@footballiq.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
