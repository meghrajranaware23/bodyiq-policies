Privacy Policy

Football IQ – Mental Performance Training App  ·  Effective: June 2025

Your privacy is fully protected. Football IQ is built on one simple principle — what happens on your phone, stays on your phone.
We Collect Zero Data Football IQ does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. No accounts. No sign-ups. No servers. Everything lives only on your device.

Who We Are

Football IQ is a mobile application that trains football decision-making and tactical intelligence. For any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@footballiq.app.

No Account, No Sign-Up

Football IQ requires no registration, login, email address, or personal details of any kind. You download the app and start training immediately. We have no way to identify who you are — and we don't want to.

All Data Stays On Your Device

Everything the app generates — your IQ scores, drill results, progress, training history, and preferences — is stored exclusively in your phone's local storage. This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it, and it is never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you uninstall the app, all of this data is permanently removed from your device.

No Internet Required

All core training features of Football IQ work fully offline. No data is sent or received during your sessions.

No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party services that collect user data. There are no ads in the app. Your behaviour inside the app is completely private.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect absolutely no data, Football IQ is safe for users of all ages including players under 13. There is nothing to collect, store, or share about any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices ever change, we will update this policy and notify users through the app before any changes take effect. The "Effective" date at the top always reflects the latest version.
