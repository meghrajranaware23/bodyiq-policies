Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data private and secure?

A: Yes! All your data (habits, workouts, calories, sleep, photos) is stored locally on your device only. We do NOT store any of your personal information on our servers. Your privacy is our top priority.

Q: How does the AI calorie estimation work?

A: Our CalAI feature uses advanced AI to analyze food photos or descriptions and estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Simply take a photo or describe what you ate, and the AI does the rest.

Q: Can I backup my data?

A: Currently, all data is stored locally. If you uninstall the app, your data will be permanently deleted. We recommend keeping the app installed to maintain your progress and streaks.

Q: How do I use the looks/self-improvement feature?

A: This optional feature analyzes selfies locally on your device to provide grooming and appearance insights. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers and are processed entirely on your device.

Q: Do I need an account to use Orbit?

A: No! Most features work without an account. We use anonymous login for basic app functionality, but you remain completely anonymous and your data stays on your device.

Q: Will I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Please visit Apple's support site to request a refund for App Store purchases.
