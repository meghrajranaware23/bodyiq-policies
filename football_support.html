Tips for Best Results

01

Train daily for short sessions — 10 minutes of focused drills beats an hour of passive watching.

02

Start on easier difficulty and increase the level once you're consistently scoring above 80%.

03

Use the app before training sessions to prime your decision-making for the pitch.

04

Review your weakest drill categories and focus extra sessions on those areas.
